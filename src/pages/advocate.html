Storm Trade
Advocates

The Advocates Program was designed to promote awareness and foster a vibrant community around Storm Trade. This comprehensive initiative includes a structured framework with progress levels, tasks, and rewards, enhancing participant engagement and deepening their connection with the project.

If you are passionate about Storm Trade, actively engaged in trading, or create high-quality educational content, you are exactly who we are looking for.

Rewards for participation

Advocates receive unique rewards for their contributions, including exclusive NFTs, access to private groups, and more. The rewards are designed to recognize and incentivize the hard work and dedication of our ambassadors.

After achieving the Sensei role, the Advocate is entitled to a reward in the form of $STORM tokens.

Tokens will be distributed monthly based on monthly reports.

Advocate Levels

Each level is designed to guide participants on their journey, recognizing their growth and rewarding their dedication as they evolve from newcomers to key contributors shaping the future of Storm Trade.

Level 1: Student

Awarded upon joining the ambassador program, this title marks the beginning of a participant's journey, introducing them to key objectives, resources, and tasks.

Requirements

  • Join Storm Trade Bot
  • Complete the basic tasks in the Onboarding category on the Zealy platform
  • Demonstrate a clear understanding of the Storm Trade and its mission

Benefits

  • Opportunity to join closed Advocates Telegram chat
  • Further participation in the Advocates Program

Level 2: Master

Masters actively complete tasks, deepen their knowledge, and build connections with peers, focusing on skill development and community engagement.

Requirements

  • Actively completes tasks and accumulates points
  • When you feel ready, fill out the form to upgrade your level

Benefits

  • Unique role on Advocates Telegram chat
  • Ability to earn Points and Rewards for Situational tasks

Level 3: Sensei

Having earned recognition in the community for their contributions, Senseis guide others with their expertise and foster a positive community environment.

Requirements

  • Active participation and interaction in official Storm Trade communities
  • Complete at least 5 pieces of content in Master role within 4 weeks in Master way [1] section
  • Complete at least 14 activity tasks in Master role within 4 weeks in Master way [2] section
  • The percentage of successfully completed tasks is at least 80%

Benefits

  • Opportunity to earn rewards for your contributions as an official Storm Trade ambassador
  • Unique role on Storm Trade community

Level 4: Grandmaster

The highest role, achieved by ambassadors who exemplify leadership, commitment, and significant influence, serving as role models within the community.

Requirements

  • Be in an Sensei position for at least 2 month
  • Provide mentorship and guidance to other ambassadors
  • Active participation and interaction in official Storm Trade communities
  • Consistently complete high-quality tasks from the task board
  • The percentage of successfully completed tasks is at least 85%

Benefits

  • Access personalized and customized assignments for rewards
  • Unique role on Storm Trade community
