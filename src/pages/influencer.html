What is CPM?

CPM - (cost per mile) is a pricing model and metric commonly used in marketing and advertising. Also called cost per thousand impressions. In Storm Trade Ambassador Program CPM refers to the total rewards for every 1,000 impressions a content piece receives.

CPM Ratio

This ratio reflects the multiplier applied to your base CPM rate for each content category. The more impactful or challenging the content type, the higher the CPM ratio, resulting in greater rewards for creating higher-quality or more strategic content.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Storm Trade Influencer Program

Are there specific requirements for the number of posts per week?

No, there are no specific requirements for weekly posting frequency. You have the flexibility to publish as many posts as you wish.

What are the main goals for ambassadors?

The main focus is on attracting traders and increasing trading volume. The team will periodically provide specific topics and directions for posts based on current priorities and initiatives.

How can I increase my chances of being selected for ambassador programs?

To increase your chances of being selected for ambassador programs, you should focus on creating high-quality content, engaging with the community, and demonstrating a strong understanding of the platform.

What is the process for becoming an ambassador?

The process for becoming an ambassador typically involves creating a profile on the platform, creating high-quality content, and engaging with the community. You may also be required to complete a short application form.

What are the benefits of being an ambassador?

The benefits of being an ambassador include receiving exclusive rewards, access to a closed community, and the opportunity to shape the future of decentralized trading.

How often should I post content?

The frequency of posting content depends on your schedule and the platform you are using. It's important to maintain a consistent posting schedule to engage with your audience effectively.

How can I measure the success of my ambassador program?

You can measure the success of your ambassador program by tracking metrics such as the number of new traders attracted, the amount of trading volume generated, and the engagement level of your community.

What is the role of an ambassador in the community?

The role of an ambassador is to promote the platform, attract new traders, share recommendations, and provide valuable insights to the community.

How can I collaborate with other ambassadors?

You can collaborate with other ambassadors by sharing ideas, content, and resources. This can help you reach a wider audience and increase the effectiveness of your ambassador program.

What is the difference between a regular user and an ambassador?

The main difference between a regular user and an ambassador is that ambassadors are actively promoting the platform and sharing their insights with the community.

How can I get started with ambassador programs?

To get started with ambassador programs, you should first create a profile on the platform, create high-quality content, and engage with the community. You may also want to join ambassador communities or groups to learn more about the program.

What is the best way to engage with the community?

The best way to engage with the community is to create high-quality content, participate in discussions, and share your insights with other members. This can help you build a strong relationship with the community and increase your chances of being selected for ambassador programs.

How can I create high-quality content?

To create high-quality content, you should focus on providing valuable insights, sharing your experiences, and demonstrating a strong understanding of the platform. You should also ensure that your content is well-written, visually appealing, and easy to understand.

I've received an invitation and joined the group. Can I start creating content right away?

Yes, you can begin creating content immediately after joining.
