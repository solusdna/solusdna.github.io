Influencer Levels

Each level is designed to guide participants on their journey, recognizing their growth and rewarding their dedication as they evolve from newcomers to key contributors shaping the future of Storm Trade.

Level 1: Explorer

Explorers are new members who are just beginning their journey with StormTrade.

Requirements

  • Have a community or audience on various social networks
  • Fill out the form

Main Tasks

  • Create and share user-generated content on social media
  • Spread awareness about Storm Trade

Benefits

  • CPM rate of x1
  • Limited access to Discord and Telegram channels
  • Opportunity to grow within the program

Level 2: Connector

Connectors are participants who have demonstrated consistent engagement and activity.

Requirements

  • Create a minimum of 5 content units with a CPM Ratio of at least 85%
  • Invite at least 7 users to the Storm Trade platform
  • Rank in the top 10 of the leaderboard

Main Tasks

  • Create in-depth analysis and posts
  • Provide feedback on program features
  • Invite other users to join Storm Trade

Benefits

  • CPM rate of x2
  • Exclusive Connectors events
  • Exclusive Connectors rewards

Level 3: Mastermind

Masterminds are the core members who have shown dedication and deep understanding.

Requirements

  • Create a minimum of 12 content units with a CPM Ratio of at least 85%
  • Invite at least 15 users to Storm Trade
  • Rank in the top 5 of the leaderboard

Main Tasks

  • Develop and lead marketing campaigns
  • Represent Storm Trade at local events
  • Mentor new participants

Benefits

  • Highest CPM rates at x3.2
  • Access to MarketMakers closed community
  • Exclusive ambassador rewards

Tasks and Evaluation

Influencers are evaluated based on the content they have created on various platforms (e.g. X, Telegram, YouTube) as well as the number of users they have attracted through their personal referral link.

What is CPM?

CPM - (cost per mile) is a pricing model and metric commonly used in marketing and advertising. Also called cost per thousand impressions. In Storm Trade Ambassador Program CPM refers to the total rewards for every 1,000 impressions a content piece receives.

CPM Ratio

This ratio reflects the multiplier applied to your base CPM rate for each content category. The more impactful or challenging the content type, the higher the CPM ratio, resulting in greater rewards for creating higher-quality or more strategic content.
